Management and Development of Software Engineering

The bachelor of management and development of software engineering has the main purpose to form professionals with abilities and knowledge in design and project management of software development like architecture systems and mobile apps. We are capable of formulating innovative proposals that contribute to highly productivity in a software company.

Candite profile

General management of IT and communication having the passion for knowledge and management of software and hardware configuration. Interested in our computing process. The capacity for observation,analysis,synthesis, creativity and working in teams.

Graduated profile

The graduated student of management and development of software engineering is characterized for a technical solid professional formation and scientific. There for it should be combined with humanistic bases. Which lets them to developed successfully in the professional field.
